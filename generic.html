About Me

EN-US

I am a skilled data analyst with a foundation in tools like SQL, Tableau, Power BI, and Excel, complemented by experience in various other data visualization and analysis platforms. My passion lies in turning complex data into actionable insights, helping businesses make informed decisions.

Born and raised in Brazil until the age of 15, I have since lived in several countries, including Portugal, Greece, and New York, California, and Florida in the United States. These diverse experiences have not only broadened my cultural understanding but also enhanced my adaptability, making me quick to adjust to new environments and challenges.

I pride myself on being a problem solver, with a natural ability to analyze situations, identify potential issues, and develop effective solutions. My time spent across various regions has honed my ability to adapt quickly, learn new skills efficiently, and communicate effectively with people from all walks of life. Additionally, I am detail-oriented, highly organized, and consistently strive for excellence in everything I do.

I am eager to leverage my technical expertise and unique life experiences in a data analysis role where I can contribute to meaningful projects and continue to grow professionally.
